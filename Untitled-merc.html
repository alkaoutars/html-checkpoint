WHAT IS Mercedes class G ?

The Mercedes-Benz G-Class, also known as the G-Wagon, is a luxury SUV with exceptional off-road capabilities and advanced technology features. Its distinct boxy shape and powerful engine make it a recognizable and impressive vehicle for both on and off-road adventures. The G-Class also boasts a spacious and luxurious cabin with premium materials and advanced driver assistance features.
